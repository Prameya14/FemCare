SDGs Impacted

Highlighting our project's impact on the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), showcasing our dedication to global progress. Through targeted efforts, we contribute to sustainable development and societal advancement.

SDG - 3

Good Health and Well-being

FemCare offers early diagnosis and risk assessments for gynecological diseases, reducing mortality and improving overall women's health and well-being through timely interventions and support.

SDG - 4

Quality Education

FemCare educates women on gynecological health, enhancing their knowledge and promoting lifelong learning, thereby contributing to inclusive and equitable quality education.

SDG - 5

Gender Equality

By addressing health issues unique to women and providing tailored resources, FemCare empowers women, promotes gender-specific healthcare, and supports the fight for gender equality in health.

SDG - 9

Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure

Utilizing advanced AI and machine learning, FemCare fosters innovation in healthcare, contributing to sustainable industrialization and building resilient health infrastructure.
